We will now check the details you have sent.

If we can find your vaccination record

If we can find your vaccination record, we will send you an NHS COVID Pass letter to the address you have registered with your GP surgery.

You should get this letter within 5 working days.

Keep this letter safe. You can use this as proof of your coronavirus (COVID-19) vaccination status.

If we cannot find your vaccination record

If we cannot find your vaccination record, you will not receive an NHS COVID Pass letter.

But we will let you know if we cannot find your vaccination record.

We will contact you by the method you selected on the previous screen.

We may not be able to find your vaccination record because:

  • you entered your details incorrectly
  • you’re not fully vaccinated
  • you did not have your vaccination in England
  • it has been less than 5 working days since your 2nd dose (or 1 dose of the Janssen vaccine) and the system has not been updated

You can try:

  • entering your details again
  • checking the details your GP surgery has for you are up to date or registering with a GP surgery if you do not have one – if you need to update your details you can try using this service again after you’ve updated them
  • wait until 5 working days after you were fully vaccinated

If you have tried these things and you still cannot get a letter, call 119.
