What are cookies

Cookies are files saved on your phone, tablet or computer when you visit a website.

They store information about how you use the website. Some of them can be turned on and off.

How we use cookies

We use cookies to:

  • make our website work
  • measure how you use our website, such as which links you click on (analytics cookies), if you give us permission
